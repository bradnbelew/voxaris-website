## SCRIPT 2: "The Saturday Night Lead"

**Use Case:** Social media (LinkedIn, Instagram Reels, TikTok), Facebook/Meta ads, YouTube pre-roll
**Duration Target:** 25–30 seconds
**Format:** Scenario-based narration — Maria speaks directly to the dealer, walking through a real-time situation

---

### SCENE DESCRIPTION

Maria is framed slightly closer — more intimate than Script 1. She's drawing the viewer into a story. The background of the V·GUIDE interface subtly shows a dealership website in the background — blurred but recognizable. The lighting is slightly warmer to reinforce the late-night scenario.

Optional B-roll or picture-in-picture effect: show a split screen — the empty dealership lot or dark BDC desk on the left, and Maria actively engaging a visitor on the right. This can be toggled on/off for different ad placements.

The pacing is deliberate. She lets the scene breathe. No rushing.

---

### ON-SCREEN TEXT SUGGESTIONS

- [0:00] Text card before she speaks: **"11:00 PM. Saturday."** — holds for 1.5 seconds, then fades to Maria
- [0:06] Subtle text overlay: **"Your BDC left at 6 PM."** (appears and fades by :08)
- [0:12] Text overlay: **"I'm here."** — small, bottom-right corner
- [0:22] Split-screen panel or B-roll: visitor browsing a 2026 Accord → Maria greeting → calendar booking animation
- [0:28] Closing CTA card: **voxaris.io/book-demo**

---

### DIALOGUE (Narrative, Cinematic — She's Telling Their Story Back to Them)

*(Pre-roll silence, 1.5 seconds — text card: "11:00 PM. Saturday." plays first)*

"It's 11pm on a Saturday.

A customer just landed on your website — they're looking at a 2026 Accord. They want to know if it comes in Sonic Gray. They want to know about the financing offer they saw on TV.

Your BDC went home 5 hours ago.

But I didn't.

I greeted them. Pulled up the trim options. Answered the financing question. And booked them a test drive for Monday at 10am — before they ever had a reason to leave.

You woke up Sunday morning to a booked appointment you didn't know you had.

That's what 24/7 really looks like.

I'm V·GUIDE. And your dealership deserves this."

*(Natural close — brief pause, warm expression, then CTA card.)*

## PRODUCTION NOTES (All Three Scripts)

**Maria AI Persona Consistency:**
- Appearance: Brunette, dark blazer, professional composure
- Interface: Dark mode, red accent glow, V·GUIDE branding bottom-right corner
- Micro-expressions: Warm eye contact, natural head movements, brief nod at close lines
- No robotic cadence — pacing should feel like a trusted colleague, not a TTS demo

**Audio:**
- No background music on Scripts 1 and 3 — the data should do the work
- Script 2 ("Saturday Night Lead") can carry a low ambient tone or subtle tension music that resolves at the "But I didn't" moment
- Ensure final mix is clear at low volume for mobile playback

**Caption Files:**
- Provide SRT files for all three scripts for Facebook, Instagram, and LinkedIn accessibility
- Caption style: white text, black rounded background, bottom-center — do not use default platform captions on LinkedIn ads

**Brand Lock:**
- Never reference third-party AI vendors in any script, demo, or creative
- V·GUIDE is proprietary Voxaris technology
- All personas, interactions, and capability references are internal IP
