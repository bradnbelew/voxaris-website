## AD PRODUCTION CHECKLIST

Before trafficking any ad:

- [ ] All creative reviewed for brand voice (warm, confident, professional — not salesy)
- [ ] Zero mentions of Tavus, Retell, or any third-party platform
- [ ] CTA buttons link to `voxaris.io/book-demo`
- [ ] UTM parameters appended to all URLs (e.g., `?utm_source=meta&utm_medium=paid_social&utm_campaign=batch1&utm_content=[ad_name]`)
- [ ] Video captions/subtitles enabled for all video ads
- [ ] All video assets in 1:1 (feed), 9:16 (stories/reels), and 16:9 (LinkedIn) formats
- [ ] Static image assets: 1080x1080 (feed), 1080x1920 (stories)
- [ ] LinkedIn ads reviewed for professional tone alignment
- [ ] Each ad set has a clear single audience — no overlap
