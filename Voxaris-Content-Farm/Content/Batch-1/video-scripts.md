# Video Scripts — Maria (AI Avatar)
**Batch:** Batch-1 | **Type:** Short-form video scripts | **Persona:** Maria — brunette, dark blazer, dark mode UI, red accents
**Platform use:** Homepage, vertical landing pages, paid ads, demo page

---

## PRODUCTION NOTES (All Scripts)

- **Maria's tone:** Warm, direct, slightly conversational. Not stiff. Not robotic. She sounds like the smartest, friendliest person in the room.
- **Visual baseline:** Dark background (#0D0D0D), subtle Voxaris branding bottom-left, red accent pulse when Maria transitions or a CTA appears.
- **Camera framing:** Medium close-up. Maria slightly right of center. Clean negative space left for on-screen text overlays.
- **Delivery pace:** Measured. Natural pauses. Not rushed.
- **Subtitles:** Always on (auto or burned-in). White text, red key-word highlights.
