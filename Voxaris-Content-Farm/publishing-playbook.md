### Platform-Specific Notes

**X (Twitter)**
- Cards (link previews with images) auto-generate when you paste a URL — no need to attach a separate image for link posts.
- Alt text on images is required for accessibility and improves reach.
- Threads: keep each tweet to one clear idea. First tweet = the hook; final tweet = the CTA.

**Instagram**
- Always design for 4:5 (portrait) ratio — it takes up more feed real estate.
- Carousel posts (up to 10 slides) receive the highest organic reach of any feed format.
- Use a consistent grid aesthetic — alternating brand colors, typography, and white space.

**TikTok**
- Hook must land in the first 1–3 seconds. Text overlay + strong visual movement.
- Trending audio increases discoverability; save a weekly shortlist of trending sounds.
- Stitch and Duet formats extend reach and encourage community interaction.

**LinkedIn**
- First line of a post is everything — make it a scroll-stopper question or bold claim.
- Personal posts from the founder account outperform company page posts 5–10×.
- Articles get indexed by Google — treat them as long-form SEO assets.

**YouTube Shorts**
- Include a verbal CTA at the 45-second mark: "Follow for part 2" or "Link in description."
- Strong thumbnail matters even for Shorts when browsed outside the Shorts feed.
- Chapters in descriptions improve long-form video SEO; not needed for Shorts.

### Platform-Specific Hashtag Rules

#### X (Twitter)
- **How many:** 1–3 max. More than 3 reduces engagement.
- **Placement:** End of post or woven naturally into a sentence.
- **Strategy:** Use 1 brand tag (`#Voxaris`) + 1 industry/trending tag. Skip hashtags on threads beyond the first tweet — they look spammy.
- **Example:** `Automating your hotel's front desk is no longer a luxury. It's table stakes. #HotelTech #Voxaris`

#### Instagram Feed & Reels
- **How many:** 3–5 (research shows diminishing returns above 5 for reach; 3–5 hits the sweet spot for non-spammy discoverability).
- **Placement:** End of caption after 2 line breaks, OR in the first comment (either works — test both).
- **Strategy:** 1 brand + 1 industry + 1–2 vertical + 1 discovery.
- **Example block:**
  ```
  #Voxaris #AIAgents #RealEstateTech #BuildInPublic
  ```

#### Instagram Stories
- **How many:** 1–2 (Hashtag sticker, not text).
- **Placement:** Use the Hashtag sticker, not typed text — sticker is clickable and boosts discovery.
- **Strategy:** Use the most relevant vertical tag only.

#### TikTok
- **How many:** 3–5.
- **Placement:** Caption after the main copy.
- **Strategy:** Mix 1 broad (#AIForBusiness or #FutureOfWork) + 1 niche vertical (#LegalTech, #HotelTech) + 1 community (#BuildInPublic or #FounderLife). Avoid using only brand tags — TikTok rewards discoverability over branding.
- **Example:** `#AIForBusiness #LegalTech #FounderLife`

#### Facebook (Feed)
- **How many:** 1–3.
- **Placement:** End of post.
- **Strategy:** Facebook hashtags have limited discovery impact. Use sparingly — brand tag + 1 broad industry tag only.
- **Example:** `#Voxaris #AIAgents`

#### Facebook Ads
- **How many:** 0.
- **Notes:** Hashtags in ad copy hurt CTR. Remove all hashtags from paid ad variants.

#### LinkedIn Feed
- **How many:** 3–5.
- **Placement:** End of post, after a line break.
- **Strategy:** 1 brand + 2 industry + 1–2 vertical. LinkedIn hashtag search is actively used by professionals.
- **Example block:**
  ```
  #Voxaris #ConversationalAI #AIForBusiness #LegalTech
  ```

#### LinkedIn Articles
- **How many:** 3 (article tags at publish, not in the body).
- **Strategy:** Use the 3 most search-relevant tags for the article topic when publishing. Do not embed hashtags in article body text — it reads as unprofessional.

#### YouTube Shorts / Videos
- **How many:** 3–5 in the description.
- **Placement:** End of description, after the CTA and links.
- **Strategy:** Mix broad (#AI2026, #AIAgents) with specific vertical for searchability.

### Detailed Weekly Schedule

#### Monday — LinkedIn Thought Leadership + Blog Publish

**8:00 AM** — Blog post goes live on voxaris.io
- Target: 1,200–2,000 words
- SEO-optimized with primary keyword in title, H1, first paragraph, meta description
- Internal links to 2+ other Voxaris pages

**10:00 AM** — LinkedIn thought leadership post
- Format: Personal founder post OR company page post
- Hook: Bold claim or counterintuitive observation from the blog
- Length: 800–1,300 characters (stops just before "see more" or intentionally triggers it with a cliffhanger)
- CTA: "Full breakdown in the blog — link in first comment"
- Hashtags: #Voxaris #ConversationalAI #AIForBusiness + 1 vertical

**Goal:** Authority positioning. Start the week as the smart voice in the AI agents conversation.

---

#### Tuesday — X Thread + Instagram Carousel

**9:00 AM** — X Thread (6–10 tweets)
- Thread structure:
  - Tweet 1: Hook (bold claim, stat, or question — make it standalone-shareable)
  - Tweets 2–8: One insight or tip per tweet; short, punchy, no filler
  - Tweet 9: Summary / "If you take nothing else from this thread..."
  - Tweet 10: CTA ("Follow @Voxaris for more" or "Reply with your use case")
- Topic: Derived from Monday's blog post
- Hashtags: 1–2 on Tweet 1 only

**12:00 PM** — Instagram Carousel Post
- 6–10 slides
- Slide 1: Bold hook headline (matches X thread topic)
- Slides 2–8: One point per slide with simple visual
- Slide 9: Key takeaway / summary
- Slide 10: CTA + @Voxaris handle
- Caption: 100–200 words, storytelling hook, 3–5 hashtags at end

**Goal:** Expand blog content to the audiences who don't read blogs. Carousels get saved and reshared.

---

#### Wednesday — Blog #2 + Email Newsletter

**8:00 AM** — Blog post #2 goes live on voxaris.io
- Different vertical focus than Monday's post
- Can be shorter (800–1,200 words) or a listicle/comparison format

**10:00 AM** — Email Newsletter
- Subject line: Under 50 characters; curiosity or value-driven
- Preview text: Complements subject line
- Structure:
  - Intro (2–3 sentences): What's in this edition
  - Block 1: Highlight from Blog #1 with link
  - Block 2: Highlight from Blog #2 with link
  - Block 3: Product tip, feature spotlight, or customer win
  - CTA: "Book a demo" or "Try Voxaris free"
- Send time: 10:00 AM EST
- Segment: All subscribers (until list is large enough to segment)

**Goal:** Drive traffic back to the site. Own the inbox relationship.

---

#### Thursday — TikTok/Reels + LinkedIn Post

**9:00 AM** — TikTok + Instagram Reels (same video, different captions)
- Length: 45–75 seconds
- Format: Talking head, screen share, or text-on-screen tutorial
- Hook: First 3 seconds must contain a verbal or visual pattern interrupt
- Structure:
  - 0:00–0:03 — Hook ("Most businesses are losing leads at this exact moment...")
  - 0:03–0:40 — Core value (3 quick points or one clear demo)
  - 0:40–0:55 — CTA ("Follow for part 2" or "Comment your vertical")
- Captions: Different for TikTok vs. Reels (TikTok = more casual/punchy; Reels = slightly more polished)

**11:00 AM** — LinkedIn Post (text-only or single image)
- Topic: Supports or teases the video topic
- Format: Short-form observation (under 500 characters) or a "controversial" industry take
- CTA: Drive to TikTok/Reels video or to book a demo

**Goal:** Build short-form video presence and algorithmic momentum on TikTok and Reels.

---

#### Friday — X Thread + Facebook Post

**9:00 AM** — X Thread
- Tone: Slightly more casual/reflective than Tuesday
- Format options: "Lessons learned this week," "Hot take," or "5 things I'd tell founders about AI agents"
- 4–7 tweets
- CTA: "What's your take? Reply below."

**12:00 PM** — Facebook Post
- Audience: Slightly older, decision-maker demographic
- Format: 1–2 short paragraphs + image or video
- Tone: Conversational, benefit-focused, not jargon-heavy
- CTA: Link to blog or demo page
- Hashtags: 1–2 max

**Goal:** Round out the week with engagement-driving content. Friday posts have lower competition and good comment rates.

---

#### Weekend — Repurpose Best Performer

**Saturday**
- Review week's analytics (30 minutes)
- Identify the post with the highest reach, engagement, or saves
- Repost or share as a Story with added context
- Optionally: Boost as a paid Facebook/Instagram Story ad ($10–50 spend)

**Sunday**
- Draft and schedule Monday's LinkedIn post and blog
- Film Thursday's video if not done during the week
- Refill the Canva/design queue with next week's carousel slides

## 4. Content Repurposing Matrix

### The 1-to-7 Voxaris Content Engine

One well-researched blog post becomes 7+ distinct content assets. This is the core efficiency principle of the Voxaris content farm.

```
                        ┌─────────────────────┐
                        │     BLOG POST        │
                        │  (1,500–2,000 words)  │
                        └──────────┬──────────┘
              ┌───────────┬────────┴──────────┬──────────────┐
              ▼           ▼                   ▼              ▼
         X Thread    LinkedIn Post       IG Carousel    TikTok Script
         (6–10 tweets) (800–1,300 chars)  (6–10 slides)  (45–75 sec)
              │                                              │
              └──────────┬────────────────────┬─────────────┘
                         ▼                    ▼
                   Email Snippet           Ad Copy
                  (200–300 words)      (Headline + 125 chars)
```

---

### Repurposing SOP: Step by Step

#### Source: Blog Post
- **Input:** 1,500–2,500 word article, 3–5 key insights, 1 primary argument

**Step 1 → X Thread**
- Extract the 6–8 most standalone, punchy insights
- Each tweet = one insight, maximum 240 characters
- Thread format: Hook → Insights → Summary → CTA
- Estimated time: 20 minutes

**Step 2 → LinkedIn Post**
- Take the blog's core argument and rewrite as a first-person perspective
- Open with a bold claim or personal observation (not "I wrote a blog about...")
- 3 short paragraphs or a bulleted list of takeaways
- End with: "Full post in the first comment. What would you add?"
- Estimated time: 15 minutes

**Step 3 → Instagram Carousel**
- Identify the blog's 6–9 key points or steps
- Each becomes one slide: headline + 1–2 sentences max
- Slide 1 = Blog headline (punched up for visual)
- Final slide = CTA + @Voxaris + website
- Design in Canva using Voxaris brand template
- Estimated time: 45–60 minutes (including design)

**Step 4 → TikTok/Reels Script**
- Distill blog to the single most surprising or actionable insight
- Write a 45–75 second script using this structure:
  - Hook: "Here's what [target audience] gets wrong about [topic]..."
  - Point 1, Point 2, Point 3 (5–10 seconds each, max)
  - CTA: "Follow for the full breakdown"
- Record using teleprompter app or bullet points
- Estimated time: 30 minutes (scripting + filming)

**Step 5 → Email Snippet**
- Pull the blog's intro paragraph (rewrite slightly for email voice)
- Add 2 bullet points summarizing the key takeaways
- End with: "Read the full post → [link]"
- Use in Wednesday newsletter as one content block
- Estimated time: 10 minutes

**Step 6 → Ad Copy (Facebook/Instagram/LinkedIn)**
- Headline (under 40 characters): Take the blog title, tighten it to the core benefit
- Primary text (125 characters): One problem/solution sentence from the blog's intro
- Description: A supporting data point or social proof line
- CTA button: "Learn More" or "Book a Demo"
- Estimated time: 15 minutes

**Step 7 → YouTube Shorts Script (Optional)**
- If the blog topic lends itself to a visual demo or step-by-step walkthrough, expand the TikTok script to 55–60 seconds with more polish
- Optimize title for YouTube search: include primary keyword in first 60 characters
- Add timestamps in description if expanding to long-form
- Estimated time: 20–30 minutes additional from TikTok script

## 5. Engagement Playbook

### DM & Comment Reply Templates

#### When Someone Comments "How does this work?"

#### When Someone Asks "Is this better than [competitor]?"
> "Happy to walk through the differences — we've got a quick comparison page that covers the key ones: [link]. The short version: [key differentiator]. What matters most to you in a solution like this?"

#### When Someone Leaves a Negative/Skeptical Comment
> "Appreciate the honest take. Totally fair to be skeptical — AI hype is everywhere. What would it take for you to believe this actually works? Genuinely asking."

### Comment Engagement Strategy

**Rule 1: Reply within 4 hours (weekdays)**
Set a twice-daily notification review: 9 AM and 4 PM. Reply to every comment on every post within 4 hours during business hours.

**Rule 2: Add value in every reply**
Never reply with just "Thanks!" Add one piece of information, ask a follow-up question, or redirect to a resource.

**Rule 3: Like + reply to replies**
When someone replies to your comment, always like their reply even if you don't respond again. It signals engagement to the algorithm.

**Rule 4: Go engage first, not just wait**
Spend 15 minutes per day proactively commenting on 5–10 posts by:
- Potential customers (hotel operators, law firm partners, real estate brokers)
- Industry influencers in AI, SaaS, and your target verticals
- Founders in complementary businesses (web designers, marketing agencies)

Format for outbound comments: One specific observation on their post + one genuine question or agreement. Never a pitch.

**Rule 5: Pin the best comment on your own posts**
On LinkedIn and Instagram, pin the most insightful or funny reply to make the comment section feel alive.

### Cross-Promotion Tactics

**Tactic 1: Collab Posts (Instagram)**
Partner with complementary SaaS founders or vertical-specific creators for Instagram Collab posts. Both audiences see the content. Target: 1 per month.

**Tactic 2: X Spaces**
Host a monthly X Space on a topic your target audience cares about (e.g., "The future of AI in real estate"). Invite 2–3 guests. Repurpose the recording as podcast-style content.

**Tactic 3: Newsletter Swaps**
Partner with newsletters in your verticals (hotel tech, legal tech, real estate) to cross-promote. Offer a sponsored blurb in your newsletter in exchange for one in theirs.

**Tactic 4: LinkedIn Comment Pods**
Build a small group (5–10 founders/marketers) who agree to meaningfully comment on each other's LinkedIn posts within the first hour of publishing. First-hour engagement dramatically boosts algorithmic reach.

**Tactic 5: TikTok Stitches**
Find trending TikToks about AI, sales automation, or business efficiency and Stitch with a Voxaris response or demo. Leverages the original video's audience.

**Tactic 6: Founder Podcast Circuit**
Identify 10 podcasts in your target verticals. Pitch a 20-minute appearance. Repurpose audio clips as Reels/Shorts. Target: 1 booking per month.
