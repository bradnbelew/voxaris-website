# Arrivia Membership Tier Comparison Guide

## Membership Tiers Overview

### Standard Tier
- Points earning rate: 1x on all bookings
- Hotel discounts: Up to 10% off retail rates
- Cruise discounts: None
- Exclusive deals: No
- Travel concierge: No
- Friends & Family sharing: No
- Quarterly travel specials: No
- VIP event access: No

### Silver Tier
- Points earning rate: 1.5x on all bookings
- Hotel discounts: Up to 15% off retail rates
- Cruise discounts: Up to 5% off
- Exclusive deals: Yes — access to member-only pricing on select destinations
- Travel concierge: No
- Friends & Family sharing: No
- Quarterly travel specials: Yes — early access to seasonal deals
- VIP event access: No

### Gold Tier
- Points earning rate: 2x on all bookings
- Hotel discounts: Up to 25% off retail rates
- Cruise discounts: Up to 10% off
- Exclusive deals: Yes — priority access to flash sales and limited inventory
- Travel concierge: Yes — dedicated advisor for trip planning
- Friends & Family sharing: Yes — share member pricing with up to 4 people
- Quarterly travel specials: Yes — first access before other tiers
- VIP event access: No

### Platinum Tier
- Points earning rate: 3x on all bookings
- Hotel discounts: Up to 40% off retail rates
- Cruise discounts: Up to 15% off
- Exclusive deals: Yes — first access to all deals plus Platinum-only inventory
- Travel concierge: Yes — priority concierge with 24/7 availability
- Friends & Family sharing: Yes — share member pricing with up to 8 people
- Quarterly travel specials: Yes — exclusive Platinum-only specials
- VIP event access: Yes — invitations to exclusive travel events and previews

## Reduced Renewal Pricing

Upgraded members receive reduced renewal rates compared to new sign-ups at the same tier. Members who upgrade during their current billing period typically save 15–25% on their first renewal at the new tier.

---

## Friends & Family Sharing Benefits

### Gold Tier Sharing
- Share member pricing with up to 4 designated travelers
- Shared travelers receive hotel discounts of up to 15%
- Shared travelers earn 1x points on their bookings

### Platinum Tier Sharing
- Share member pricing with up to 8 designated travelers
- Shared travelers receive hotel discounts of up to 25%
- Shared travelers earn 1.5x points on their bookings
- Shared travelers get access to quarterly specials

---

## Important Notes

- All savings and discounts are estimates based on typical availability. Actual savings vary by destination, season, and availability.
- Pricing differences are approximate annual ranges and may vary by brand program.
- Promotional offers are subject to availability and may change.
- Points earning rates apply to qualifying bookings made through the membership platform.
